1. Definition:
Asthma is a chronic inflammatory airway disease causing episodic airflow obstruction.

2. Common Triggers:
- Dust
- Pollen
- Cold air
- Exercise
- Stress

3. Symptoms:
- Wheezing
- Chest tightness
- Coughing
- Shortness of breath

4. Treatment:
- Short-acting beta agonists (SABA)
- Inhaled corticosteroids
- Long-acting bronchodilators
- Avoiding triggers

5. Emergency Indicators:
- Inability to speak in full sentences
- Blue lips or face
- Peak flow < 50% of normal
