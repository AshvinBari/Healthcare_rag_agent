# Diabetes Type 2 – Basic Clinical Guidelines

1. Definition:
Type 2 Diabetes is a metabolic disorder characterized by insulin resistance and impaired insulin secretion.

2. Symptoms:
- Frequent urination
- Increased thirst
- Fatigue
- Slow wound healing
- Blurred vision

3. Diagnosis Criteria:
- Fasting glucose ≥ 126 mg/dL
- HbA1c ≥ 6.5%
- 2-hour OGTT ≥ 200 mg/dL

4. Treatment:
- Lifestyle changes (diet, exercise)
- First-line medication: Metformin
- Additional therapies: GLP-1 agonists, SGLT2 inhibitors
- Insulin therapy for uncontrolled cases

5. Complications:
- Neuropathy
- Retinopathy
- Cardiovascular disease
- Kidney failure
