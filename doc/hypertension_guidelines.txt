1. Definition:
Hypertension is defined as persistent blood pressure above 140/90 mmHg.

2. Symptoms:
- Headache
- Chest pain
- Dizziness
- Vision problems

3. Diagnosis:
- At least 2 elevated readings on different days
- Ambulatory BP monitoring recommended

4. Treatment:
- Lifestyle modifications: salt reduction, exercise, weight loss
- Medications:
  - ACE inhibitors
  - ARBs
  - Calcium channel blockers
  - Diuretics

5. Emergency Signs:
- Severe headache
- Shortness of breath
- Chest pain
- BP above 180/120 mmHg
